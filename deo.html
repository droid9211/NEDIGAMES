Ready to take on the hottest dragon game to build a dragon city and save it from enemies?
Dragon Era Online is an 3d addictive MMORPG online strategy game loaded with amazing and realistic graphics. It’s a role playing multiplayer game, which is full of ... challenges and monster legends. The main objective of the game is to build the most awesome guild zone but while doing so, you need to be very careful as other players want to kill you. you need to build a castle, summon opponents, nature dragon, growing foods, collect resources, and ally with your team to win this adventurous game. This game is filled with a lot of fun and fantastic graphics experience. You have to develop your civilization and let people know your worth. You are not alone here, you got some outstanding opponents, who are stronger and more advanced than you so that you get some effortless strategy gaming experience. As you go further in this action game, you will encounter some of the finest missions that will lead you to the highest peak of this extraordinary drogon game.
The best thing about this action game is its completely free, means you can play it as much as you want!
